Lengendary Heroes: Mario and Link!

Super Mario World

Super Mario World is often considered one of the best games in the series and is cited as one of the greatest video games ever made. It sold more than twenty million copies worldwide, making it the best-selling SNES game. It also led to an animated television series of the same name and a 1995 sequel, Yoshi's Island.

Zelda Tears of Kingdom

Tears of the Kingdom received acclaim. Critics praised the expanded world, new powers, scale, and story; criticism focused on its voice acting and technical performance. It sold more than 10 million copies in its first three days of release and over 20.61 million copies by March 2024, making it one of the best-selling games on the Nintendo Switch.
